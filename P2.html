Problem-2

Given a positive integer n >= 1, print the natural numbers 1 to n in binary form, each in an individual line.
Constriants: Use queues
Input format: The first line contains an integer m that indicates the number of test cases, followed by positive numbers a 1 , . . . , a m .
Output format: For each positive number a i , print 1 to a i in individual lines.

Sample Input

2
3
10

Sample Output


1
10
11
1
10
11
100
101
110
