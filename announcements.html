LAB-EXAM (Mid-1)

  1. Contest will start exactly at 6:30 PM and end at 8:00 PM. After 8:00 PM you will have a grace period of 15 minutes. During this time any successful submissions will peanalised by 40 percent.
  2. You will have 4 Questions in total. 3 Questions of 10 marks each and 1 BONUS Question of 5 marks. You can only go to the BONUS question iff you have completed all the 3 Questions.
  3. Make sure you DO NOT copy any of the codes. If any copy case is found severe actions will be taken. No excuses will be entertained in this regard.
  4. If you have any doubts, call the invigilator nearby in your lab.
  5. You can submit maximum of 30 solutions for a given problem.
